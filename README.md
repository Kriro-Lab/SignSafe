# SignSafe

SignSafe is a simple tool that helps users identify hidden risks, liability clauses, and payment terms in contracts before they sign.

## How it works

Users paste their agreement into a form, and SignSafe highlights potential risks and important clauses to be aware of.

## Purpose

The goal is to give users a clearer understanding of what they are agreeing to before signing any contract.

## Disclaimer

This tool provides general information only and does not constitute legal advice.
